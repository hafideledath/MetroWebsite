Metro Usage

The Dubai Metro is one of the world's most advanced transportation systems. It is a driverless Metro that operates using 750 volts of direct current.

Metros contain an ATM which allows users to check their nol balance, as well as top up their cards.

The Metro contains multiple cabins- including a cabin for women and children, and a cabin for gold class members only. The gold class contains a special view of the front of the Metro, as well as has special seats with tray-tables.

The Metro is used for a variety of purposes, from sightseeing to commuting. On any given day, the Metro handles over 350,000 passengers.

In order to use the Metro, users must first have a nol card with a minimum balance of 7.5 AED. More information about nol cards can be found in the nol card section of this website.
