Lines

The Dubai Metro currently has 47 stations and 2 lines- red and green. The red line spans across the Sheikh Zayed road, and the green line goes around the creek.

The RTA has a journey planner which allows users to state a start and end point, and get a route between the two points. The RTA's journey planner can be a good tool when starting to familiarize with the Metro.
