nol Card Usage

There are many different varieties of nol cards- red tickets (which are disposable and do not have the benefits of other nol cards), silver cards, gold cards, and personal cards.

nol cards are versatile and aren't restricted to the Metro. They can be used for the tram, abra, ferry, water taxi, water bus, taxi, bus, and certain retail stores.

Card Type Red ticket Silver card Gold card Personal card
Usage Metro, tram and bus All RTA transportation, not including gold class, and some retail stores All RTA transportation including gold class, and some retail stores All RTA transportation, not including gold class, and some retail stores
Validity 90 days from issue, limited to 10 journeys 5 years from purchase 5 years from purchase 5 years from issue

All in all, nol cards are a versatile type of contactless payment that apply not just to the Metro, but also all RTA transport vehicles and some retail environments.
